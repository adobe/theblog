Pick your tags

Use this page to pick the tags for your post. Tags will correctly categorize your post on the blog, and ensure we can pull metrics for it.

Once you click on a tag, it will populate at the bottom of the page. When you have picked all the tags, click “copy” and then paste the information directly in your blog post draft.

  • Blog categories = will place your article in the right topic area per the main blog menu
  • Products = ensures your post shows when readers use the product dropdown filter on blog pages. Pick your product and its parent; for example, Adobe Sign and Document Cloud.
  • Industries = use only when your post includes mention of an industry; this is not a catchall for audience targeting
  • Internal = Required for measurement. You must choose a Corporate Alignment tag.
